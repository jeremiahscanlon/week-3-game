JavaScript Word Guess

Welcome to our JavaScript Word Guess game. We are trying to be politically correct and child safe here. As a result we decided not to name the site after the evil capital punishment game that has been defiling children's souls for ages.

  • A word is chosen at random from a list.
  • The number of letters of the word is represented by the spaces in the word section.
  • Guess a letter by hitting a key on your keyboard.
  • If you guess correctly the letter will show in it's spot in the word section.
  • You have ten wrong guesses before you lose.
  • Non-letters and anything you guessed already will not count as a wrong guess.
  • You can see how many wrong guess you have and letters you need to finish the word in the stats section.
  • To restart the game simply refresh the page.
  • NOTE: If you are on mobile please use the entry box below.
